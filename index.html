Computer Scientist:

I am currently completing an M.S. in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin - Madison (est. Winter 2022). My main areas of interest are in Machine Learning/Artificial Intellegence and Data Science/Big Data. If you are interested in the course work that I have completed please check out my Acedemics Page!

As an example of such crousework, for my Data Visualization class I created a tool: Sub Group Explorer Tool in an effort to address a very common issue in the data science community currently, which is finding interesting sub groups within a large dataset. It takes as input a dataset and outputs multivariate visualizations of sub groups. The tool can be used in an exploritory manner to look at the statistical distributions and correlation between data, as well as a visualization tool once the interesting relationships have been determined.
